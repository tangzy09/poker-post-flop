Poker Equity Calculator

Enter two Texas Hold'em ranges and an optional board to get the exact win probability, computed by Monte-Carlo simulation right in your browser. Free, no signup.

How to use it

Type ranges in standard poker shorthand, separated by commas:

  • Pairs: QQ+ (queens and up), 22-66 (a band)
  • Suited: A2s+, T8s, 76s
  • Offsuit: KJo+, AQo

Add a board like Kh 7d 2c (flop), plus a turn and river card for later streets, to get post-flop equity. Leave the board blank for a preflop range-vs-range matchup. Cards are two characters: rank (2-9, T, J, Q, K, A) + suit (s, h, d, c).

FAQ

How does the equity calculator work?

It runs a Monte-Carlo simulation — dealing tens of thousands of random runouts consistent with your ranges and any board you enter, then reporting how often each side wins. The numbers are computed live, never looked up or invented.

What range notation does it accept?

Standard shorthand: pairs like QQ+ or 22-66, suited hands like A2s+ or T8s, offsuit like KJo+, comma-separated. Add a board (Kh 7d 2c) for post-flop equity; leave it blank for preflop.

Is it free?

Yes — completely free, no signup, and it runs entirely in your browser.
