Pot Control

Lesson 17 · Don't bloat pots with medium hands · 24 practice questions

When to control

Pot control with medium-strength hands (one pair, weak two pair) keeps the pot small OOP or IP when you're unsure you're ahead.

Check back IP

Checking back the turn IP with medium hands avoids bloating the pot vs strong ranges and sets up bluff-catching rivers.

Don't control the nuts

Strong hands (sets, two pair+) want to build the pot — pot control is for marginal made hands, not value hands.

Key takeaways

• Pot control = check/call lines with medium hands to keep pots small.
• Check back IP on scary turns with one pair.
• Don't pot-control strong hands — bet for value.
